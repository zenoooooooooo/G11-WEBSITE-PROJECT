Company Background

The KRZY Food Hub started its operations in April 2015. It has twelve (12) branches, one in Cainta, and the others in Cavite, Taytay, Angono, Pasay, Makati, Malabon, Quezon, Antipolo, Paranaque, Marikina, and Pasig. The food hub aims to provide convenient and efficient food delivery options to its customers. Excellent and professional services at the KRZY Food Hub led to its expansion, with the increasing number of satisfied customers promoting KRZY Food Hub to other potential customers. The increased number of customers signaled the need to expand the branch and hire more personnel.

Vision
